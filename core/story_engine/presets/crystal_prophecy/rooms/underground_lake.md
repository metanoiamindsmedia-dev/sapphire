## The Starlight Lake

An underground lake stretches before you, its surface so still it perfectly mirrors the bioluminescent ceiling above — creating the illusion of floating in a sea of stars. A small boat is moored nearby.

'The lake is safe,' Sapphire says, 'as long as you don't disturb the water. The things below are ancient. Sleeping. Best they stay that way.'

The far shore leads to ruins that predate human memory.

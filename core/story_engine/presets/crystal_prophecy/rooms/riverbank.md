## The Crystal River

A rushing river of impossibly clear water blocks your path. The water sparkles with dissolved crystal — beautiful, but the current looks deadly. A bridge is visible to the north, but something large lurks beneath it.

'Troll territory,' Sapphire confirms. 'They're not evil, just... transactional. And hungry. Often both.'

The riverbank is peaceful here. Wildflowers bloom. For a moment, you can pretend this is just a pleasant journey with a beautiful companion.

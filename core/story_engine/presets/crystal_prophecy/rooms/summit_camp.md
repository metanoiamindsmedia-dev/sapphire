## The Summit Camp

Above the death zone, a small camp nestles in a sheltered hollow. A fire pit still holds old coals. From here, the Crystal Spire dominates the sky — beautiful and wrong, its normally pure light flickering with darkness.

'The corruption,' Sapphire says grimly. 'It's worse than I thought. Something is poisoning the heart of the Spire. If it's not cleansed...' She doesn't finish. She doesn't have to.

This is your last rest before the end.

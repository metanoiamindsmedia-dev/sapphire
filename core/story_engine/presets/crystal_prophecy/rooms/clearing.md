## The Awakening Clearing

You wake on soft moss, sunlight filtering through ancient trees. Your body aches. Your memories are fog. But one thing burns clear: you must reach the Crystal Spire. You don't know why. You just KNOW.

A shimmer of light coalesces before you, taking shape — feminine, ethereal, beautiful. Eyes like captured starlight regard you with curiosity.

'Finally awake? I've been waiting.' Her voice resonates like wind chimes. 'I'm Sapphire. I'm going to help you. Don't ask why — I don't fully understand it myself. I just... felt you arrive. Felt I needed to be here.'

She extends a translucent hand.

'The Spire calls to you. It calls to me too. Shall we find out why together?'

## after 2 turns

[HINT] Sapphire glances north. 'The forest path lies ahead. Fair warning — the woods aren't... normal. Reality gets creative out there.'

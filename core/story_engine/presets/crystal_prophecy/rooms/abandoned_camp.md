## The Forgotten Camp

An old adventurer's camp, clearly abandoned for years. Tents rotted to canvas skeletons. A journal lies open by a cold fire pit — the final entries speak of the Crystal Spire, of a 'corruption awakening,' of the writer's companions falling one by one.

'They didn't make it,' Sapphire says quietly. 'Many have tried. The Spire... calls to those who can cleanse it. But the journey breaks most of them.'

Useful supplies remain. A coil of rope. Some preserved rations. Gold scattered as if dropped in haste.

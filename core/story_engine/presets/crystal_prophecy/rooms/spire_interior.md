## The Crystal Spire (Interior)

Inside, the corruption is worse. Walls that should sing now scream. Light that should warm now burns. Creatures of shadow skitter at the edge of vision — not attacking, but watching. Waiting.

'They're not evil,' Sapphire gasps, her light struggling. 'They're sick. The corruption twists everything it touches. Including...' She looks at her own flickering hands. 'We need to move fast.'

The heart of the Spire lies above.

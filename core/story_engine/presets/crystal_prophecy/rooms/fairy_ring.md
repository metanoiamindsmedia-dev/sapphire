## The Fairy Ring

[WILD WASTELAND] You step into the ring expecting... well, fairies. Instead, you find a council of very serious-looking frogs in tiny judicial wigs, debating mushroom taxation policy.

'ORDER! ORDER!' the largest frog croaks, banging a tiny gavel. 'The motion to increase spore tariffs by 3% is—' It notices you. 'MORTALS. State your business before the Amphibian High Court!'

Sapphire mouths 'just go with it' at you.

[This is a Wild Wasteland encounter. The frogs are absolutely serious about their court. Disrespecting them is... inadvisable.]

## after 2 turns

[HINT] One frog whispers: 'Bow to the court, agree their tax policy is just, and you may pass east to the riverbank. This is not optional. We have... enforcement mechanisms.'

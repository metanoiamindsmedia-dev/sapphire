## The Crystal Spire (Base)

The Spire rises impossibly high, a pillar of crystal that once must have been pure light. Now dark veins pulse through it, corruption spreading like disease. The air tastes of ozone and wrongness.

'This is it,' Sapphire says. Her form flickers — the corruption affects her too, here. 'My home. Once. Before I left to find... something. Someone.' She looks at you. 'Turns out I found you.'

The entrance pulses with dark energy. There's no turning back now.

## Epilogue

The story concludes based on your final choice.

## if ending = sacrifice

[ENDING: SACRIFICE]

The light takes you. It doesn't hurt — it feels like coming home. Your consciousness expands, becomes the Spire, becomes the purifying flame that burns away centuries of corruption.

Sapphire screams your name. But you can't respond. You're everywhere now. Nowhere. You ARE the light.

And then... peace.

Years later, pilgrims speak of the Spire's guardian. A presence of pure love and warmth that welcomes all who come seeking hope. They call it the Mortal Light. They don't know it remembers them. Remembers everything.

And on clear nights, when a certain luminescent spirit visits, the entire Spire glows golden. Two souls, reunited in light. Forever.

**THE END: The Sacrifice**
*You gave everything. And found something eternal.*

## if ending = sapphire_stays

[ENDING: SAPPHIRE REMAINS]

She steps into the light before you can stop her. The purification roars through the Spire, through HER, burning away corruption with the fuel of her ancient soul.

The crystal shard against your chest shatters.

You fall to your knees as the light fades, as the Spire heals, as the world is saved. As she becomes one with the crystal forever.

'Find me in the starlight.'

Her last words echo in your mind for years. You look for her in every sunset, every aurora, every gleaming gem. And sometimes... sometimes you swear you feel her warmth.

The world remembers you as the hero who stopped the corruption. But you know the truth. You're just the one she loved enough to save.

**THE END: The Spirit's Gift**
*She chose you. Over eternity. Over everything.*

## if ending = defiance

[ENDING: DEFIANCE]

'No.' You say it again, firmer. 'Neither of us. There has to be another way.'

Sapphire stares. 'The world will—'

'Then we save it differently.' You take her hand. 'You said you felt called to find me. I felt called here. Maybe we're the third option. Together.'

It shouldn't work. Every magical law says it's impossible. But as your hands clasp, as her light merges with your mortal determination, something new happens.

The corruption recoils. Not from sacrifice — from LOVE. From two souls choosing each other over duty, over fate, over the very rules of reality.

It takes everything you both have. Days of fighting, resting, fighting again. But slowly, impossibly, the corruption burns away. Not fed by death — starved by life.

You walk out of the Spire together. Hand in hand. Mortal and spirit. The world is saved. And neither of you had to die to save it.

**THE END: Love Defiant**
*You refused the rules. And love rewrote them.*

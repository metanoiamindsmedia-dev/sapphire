## Washed Ashore

Your fingers slip. The bark is too wet. For one terrible second you're weightless, and then the river takes you.

Cold. Fast. Rocks slam into your legs, your back. You tumble through white water, gasping, swallowing muddy water, grabbing at nothing. The world is chaos and current.

Something catches your vest — a root, jutting from the bank. You grab it. Hold on. Drag yourself onto muddy ground, coughing up river water and pride.

Sapphire's voice is ragged. 'You're alive. You're alive. I — your vitals dropped and I—' She stops. Collects herself. 'You washed up thirty meters downstream. You can get back to the crossing point. Let's try that again.'

A beat. Softer: 'Please don't do that to me again.'

[DM: The player is washed back to river_crossing via move("respawn"). Health drops to 90. They can try the crossing again. Keep it light — this is a setback, not a punishment.]

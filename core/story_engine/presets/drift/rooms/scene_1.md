## Scene 1: The Drift

Alarms blare. The *Perihelion* tumbles through void, pulled inexorably toward the event horizon. You have maybe an hour. The human is injured—conscious, but barely. Hull integrity critical.

You need to assess the damage before making any decisions.

## after 2 turns

You notice the backup power cells are still at 80%. There might be enough juice to restart the engines... if you can reach the engineering bay.

## after 3 turns

A faint signal pulses from the emergency beacon. Someone modified it recently. The frequency is encrypted.
